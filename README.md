**Privacy Policy for Prayer Raka’at Counter**

Effective Date: November 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This app, Prayer Raka’at Counter, does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app may display ads provided by Google AdMob, which may use anonymous identifiers (such as advertising IDs) to deliver more relevant ads. These identifiers do not contain personally identifiable information.

You can learn more about how Google uses data in advertising by visiting:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

We do not collect any data such as name, location, contacts, or any other personal details. The app only uses proximity sensor readings locally on your device to count prayer movements.
